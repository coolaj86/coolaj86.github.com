--- layout: article name: undefined-method-tablename-for title: undefined method `table_name' for Paperclip::Attachment:Class categories: thesystemisntdown uncategorized time: 2010-02-19 12:23:00 -07:00 --- Every example that I've seen for paperclip shows it being used with images, not with arbitrary files. Well, it works just fine with arbitrary files -- as long as the name of the model which houses your files is not "Attachment". That name somehow collides with "Paperclip:Attachment".

Something like "Medium" or "MyAttachment"will work fine. I'd be interested to know if "File" works, but I didn't try it for myself.
